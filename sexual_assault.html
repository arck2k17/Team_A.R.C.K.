Sexual Assault

The rate of sexual assault and rape has dropped by 63% in the last 20 years however, this statistic doesn’t fully explain the role that sexual assault has in every individual's life when every 98 seconds, an American is sexually assaulted and every 8 minutes, that victim is a child. What can be done if you are one of these victims and want mental or physical help from professionals.

Resources

  • What to do if you Have Been Raped
    • Pandora’s Project is a resource and support website for survivors of rape and sexual assault. If you have experienced either and don’t know what to do after being assaulted, read on to find out what steps you can take to get help. You can also call 800.656.HOPE (4673) to connect with a trained staff member from a sexual assault service provider in your area from the National Sexual Assault Telephone Hotline.

  • Help for Rape Victims, Rape Victims Support
    • If you or a loved one is seeking support after being raped, whether it be in person counselling, an online chat, or hearing other people’s experiences, you can find a list of resources that tailor to your needs.
