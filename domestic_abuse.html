Domestic Abuse

If you don’t feel safe at home and your relationship has turned abusive, please refer to the following resources to find ways to leave your abusive partner.

Resources

  • What is Domestic Violence?
    • Domestic Abuse often happens gradually and to the individual experiencing it, it becomes hard to identify when it’s happening to you. The National Coalition Against Domestic Violence explains precisely what domestic violence is and how to identify it.

  • Path to Safety
    • If you’re experiencing domestic abuse, the most important thing to do is make sure that you are safe. While that can be hard if you stay with your abusive partner, there are ways in which you and your children, pets, etc., can stay safe.
