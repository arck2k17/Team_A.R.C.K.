Double Standards

“She’s too aggressive, what a bitch!”, “Did you see her talking to all those guys last night? She’s such a slut”. Does any of this sound familiar? There are a countless number of double standards against women and every woman has heard at least five of them in her lifetime but in most cases, we’ve probably heard them all. As opposed to sitting in silence or just letting these ridiculous insults slap you in the face, find out how you can fight back.
