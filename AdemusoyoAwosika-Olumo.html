Spotlight Woman: Ademusoyo Awosika Olumo

Technology Analyst

1. What has been the biggest challenge at work and how did you overcome it?

The biggest challenge at work so far is trying to find my place while being so junior at the firm. It’s been (and still is) an adjustment.

2. Can you name a few things that have helped you succeed at work?

Networking, having honest conversations with my manager, and my mentoring relationships.

3. What has been the most satisfying thing about your job?

When I solve a coding problem that my team wasn’t sure how to solve; it’s honestly the best feeling.

4. Have you witnessed any discrimination at work and how did you handle it?

I haven’t really experienced any discrimination. I have however experienced the feeling of self-consciousness at the work place because I was a minority. I handled it by taken the weekend to regroup as well as talk to my mentors.

5. What are your hobbies and how do you incorporate them into your work?

I enjoy reading, writing, and exercising. I try to incorporate them before and after work as an opportunity to relax.

6. Lauren showed me your blog. What made you decide to create the blog?

Wow that’s so great that she showed you! My friend had asked me if I would want to be a part of it with her. The reason why I became so inspired and so passionate about creating the blog is because there isn’t really a space for women of color in STEM and often times I feel like they are an ignored demographic as people. The team and I feel that creating a space that can inspire, encourage and uplift, other young women in the STEM industry.
