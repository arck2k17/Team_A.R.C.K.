About Us

At A.R.C.K, we try to address issues that are universal to all women, so hopefully there is an article that will fit at least one of your needs. We are a website made for women by women. Our team is made up of 4 teenagers: Aliza, Radiah, Claudia, and Kelly (hence our name A.R.C.K). However like an arch, our goal is to be a safe haven for women to find support for the countless issues that we face daily. Aside from coding our website and finding content for you to read, we like to get bubble tea together and complain about the NYC subways (because it sucks am I right?).
