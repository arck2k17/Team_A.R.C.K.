Gender Pay Gap

Pay equity between females and males has been a recurring issue that has yet to be universally solved. The work of females, who hold the same title as her male counterpart and perform the same tasks, are unfortunately deemed inferior. If you’re a victim to this inequality, learn about ways in which you can bridge the gap.
